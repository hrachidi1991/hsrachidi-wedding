The background should feature a soft warm white textured paper surface, slightly off-white (ivory-white #F8F7F4), with a subtle handmade cotton paper texture. The surface must have a very light natural grain, delicate fiber details, and gentle uneven paper waves to give a luxurious fine-art print feel. The texture should be realistic, matte, and softly illuminated with diffused natural light.

Add very soft shadows around the edges to create a subtle floating card effect, as if the paper is placed over a light neutral grey backdrop.

Floral Design:
Place delicate botanical arrangements only in the top left corner and bottom right corner.

The florals must include:
• Soft lavender flowers in muted pastel purple tones (#B8A9D9, #C7BEE8)
• Small light blue wildflowers
• Thin green stems in sage green (#9CAF88)
• Fine eucalyptus-style leaves
• Baby’s breath style micro flowers

Add natural wheat stalks mixed into the floral arrangement:
• Thin golden wheat stems in soft beige-gold (#D8C59F)
• Light feathery wheat heads
• Slight natural curve for elegance
• Blended subtly with the lavender stems

The floral elements should look like watercolor botanical illustrations — soft, airy, hand-painted, semi-transparent edges — not overly saturated, not bold, not heavy.

Composition Rules:
• Keep the center 75% completely empty and clean for text placement.
• Florals should gently flow inward but never dominate the page.
• No frame border.
• No typography.
• No circles, no icons, no placeholders.
• Clean luxury minimalist aesthetic.
• Romantic, timeless, soft wedding mood.
• Fine art, realistic paper texture.
• High resolution, print quality, vertical layout (4:5 or A4 ratio).

Lighting:
Soft natural daylight from top left, very subtle shadows, no harsh contrast.

Style Keywords:
luxury minimalist wedding, botanical watercolor corner design, textured handmade paper, soft romantic aesthetic, neutral ivory background, lavender and wheat botanical arrangement, fine art print quality, elegant negative space.
